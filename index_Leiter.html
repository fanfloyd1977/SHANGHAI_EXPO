Our Technology

ACTIV-In Dot Technology

A novel platform for the preparation of microencapsulated active ingredients (AIs) and the fabrication of topical patches using laser printing technology. Enables precise AI dosage customization — greater than 90% accuracy — at 600 DPI resolution, with high-speed fabrication of 30 ms per sheet.

Customizable patch shape, size (up to A4), backing substrate type and thickness. Can integrate directly with skin analyzers for truly personalized topical patch solutions.

"Selected bioactive compounds sourced from Thailand — curcumin, aloin, and oxyresveratrol — were successfully encapsulated and fabricated into patch prototypes with strong B2B commercialization potential."

How It Works

Fabrication in 3 Steps

1
⚗️
Microencapsulated AI Toner
Active ingredients are microencapsulated into a printable powder — functioning as a precision toner for the laser printing system.
2
🔴
OPC + Laser → Latent Image
Laser activates the OPC drum to create a precise latent image. RGB grayscale directly encodes AI dosage.
3
🩹
Single Layer Deposition
Microencapsulated AIs fuse to the backing substrate at 600 DPI precision — a finished topical patch in just 30 ms/sheet.
